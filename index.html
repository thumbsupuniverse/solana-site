What are ThumbsUp?

ThumbsUp Universe is a collection of 2002 generative NFTs on the Solana network. It consists of eight different attributes that make them unique and personal.

ThumbsUp Universe is a universe developed around the platform game ThumbsUp: Endangered Universe (In Development) about two different tribes that have fought each other for years and are putting the universe in danger.
